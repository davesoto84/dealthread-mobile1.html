Peruvian Restaurant Expansion — Tampa Bay Market

Marco Reyes · Restaurant & Food · Feb 14, 2026 Verified
Target Raise

Committed

Min. Entry

67% funded · 8 investors committed⏱ 18 days left
Marco Reyes Verified
Entrepreneur · Tampa, FL · Member since 2023
St. Petersburg, Florida —

A profitable Peruvian restaurant is seeking expansion capital to open a second location in Tampa's Westshore district — one of Florida's fastest-growing dining corridors.

The business has operated continuously since 2019, generating $420K in annual revenue with a 22% net margin. The expansion targets a 1,800 sq ft space already under letter of intent in Westshore, with build-out costs of $95K and $85K in working capital for the first six months.

The owner brings 12 years of culinary and operations experience. The brand holds a 4.8-star Google rating across 380+ reviews and strong community recognition in the St. Pete market.

Use of Funds
  • Leasehold improvements and kitchen equipment — $75,000
  • Furniture, fixtures and signage — $20,000
  • Initial food inventory and supplies — $10,000
  • Working capital reserve (6 months) — $75,000
Deal Terms

Investors receive an 8% revenue share on gross monthly sales until 1.5× return, converting to a 15% equity stake in the second-location entity. Projected payback: 28–34 months.
